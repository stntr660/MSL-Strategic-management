# Strategic Alliance Management Course Syllabus & Index
## Comprehensive Academic Program Structure

---

## Course Information

**Course Title**: Strategic Alliance Management  
**Academic Level**: Postgraduate/MBA/Executive MBA  
**Duration**: 27 Modules (Full Academic Semester)  
**Credits**: 6 ECTS / 3 US Credits  
**Prerequisites**: Strategic Management Fundamentals  

**Primary Text**: Tjemkes, B., Vos, P., & Burgers, K. (2023). *Strategic Alliance Management* (3rd ed.). Routledge.

---

## Learning Philosophy

This course adopts a **Theory-Practice Integration** approach, combining:
- **Academic Rigor**: Evidence-based theoretical frameworks
- **Practical Application**: Real-world case studies and decision-making tools
- **Contemporary Relevance**: Digital transformation and sustainability considerations
- **Global Perspective**: Cross-cultural and international alliance management

---

## Part I: Core Alliance Development Framework
*Foundation Modules 1-9: The Eight-Stage Alliance Lifecycle*

### Module 1: Strategic Alliance Management [[01_Strategic_Alliance_Management.md]]
**Duration**: 2 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Foundational concepts and theoretical framework

#### Key Topics
- Alliance definition and scope
- Alliance activity growth and strategic importance
- Alliance failure causes and prevention
- Eight-stage development framework introduction
- Theoretical perspectives overview

#### Learning Outcomes
- Define strategic alliances using academic precision
- Analyze alliance growth trends and strategic implications
- Evaluate advantages/disadvantages of alliance strategies
- Apply foundational concepts to real-world scenarios

#### Assessments
- **Quiz**: Alliance definition and concepts (10%)
- **Case Analysis**: Alliance failure examination (15%)

---

### Module 2: Alliance Strategy Formulation [[02_Alliance_Strategy_Formulation.md]]
**Duration**: 3 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Governance mode decisions and theoretical foundations

#### Key Topics
- Three prototypical governance modes (make/buy/ally)
- Seven theoretical perspectives (TCE, RBV, RDP, SMT, SNT, OLP, IT)
- Governance mode rationales and decision criteria
- Five-step alliance strategy formulation framework
- Multi-theoretical integration

#### Learning Outcomes
- Distinguish between governance modes systematically
- Apply theoretical perspectives to governance decisions
- Synthesize multi-theoretical insights for strategic choices
- Design alliance strategy formulation processes

#### Assessments
- **Theoretical Analysis**: Governance mode decision (20%)
- **Framework Application**: Strategy formulation case (15%)

---

### Module 3: Alliance Partner Selection [[03_Alliance_Partner_Selection.md]]
**Duration**: 2 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Partner fit assessment and selection processes

#### Key Topics
- Resource complementarity vs. partner compatibility
- Five types of partner fit (strategic, organizational, operational, cultural, human)
- Five-step partner selection framework
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
- Partner profile development

#### Learning Outcomes
- Differentiate between complementarity and compatibility
- Apply systematic partner fit assessment frameworks
- Design comprehensive partner selection processes
- Evaluate and mitigate partner-related risks

#### Assessments
- **Partner Assessment**: Fit analysis exercise (15%)
- **Selection Framework**: Partner profile design (10%)

---

### Module 4: Alliance Negotiation
**Duration**: 2 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Negotiation behavior and valuation methods

#### Key Topics
- Negotiation behavior patterns and strategies
- Alliance valuation methodologies
- Information exchange and transparency
- Contract term negotiation
- Win-win outcome achievement

#### Learning Outcomes
- Analyze negotiation behaviors and their effectiveness
- Apply valuation methods to alliance opportunities
- Design systematic negotiation processes
- Manage information sharing and competitive dynamics

---

### Module 5: Alliance Design
**Duration**: 3 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Governance forms and structural configuration

#### Key Topics
- Governance form selection (equity vs. non-equity)
- Alliance contract specification
- Management control mechanisms
- Structural configuration options
- Design-performance relationships

#### Learning Outcomes
- Select appropriate governance forms for specific contexts
- Design comprehensive alliance contracts
- Implement effective control mechanisms
- Configure alliance structures for optimal performance

---

### Module 6: Alliance Launch
**Duration**: 2 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Implementation and early-stage management

#### Key Topics
- Launch objectives and critical success factors
- Integration planning and execution
- Early-stage relationship building
- Communication system establishment
- Performance metric initialization

#### Learning Outcomes
- Plan and execute effective alliance launches
- Manage integration challenges and opportunities
- Establish robust communication and coordination systems
- Implement initial performance monitoring

---

### Module 7: Alliance Management
**Duration**: 3 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Ongoing management approaches and systems

#### Key Topics
- Alliance management philosophies and approaches
- Coordination mechanisms and processes
- Conflict resolution and problem-solving
- Trust building and relationship maintenance
- Continuous improvement and adaptation

#### Learning Outcomes
- Apply various alliance management approaches
- Design effective coordination and control systems
- Manage conflicts and relationship challenges
- Optimize ongoing alliance performance

---

### Module 8: Alliance Evaluation
**Duration**: 2 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Performance measurement and assessment

#### Key Topics
- Performance metric design and selection
- Evaluation methodologies and frameworks
- Success measurement challenges
- Benchmarking and comparative analysis
- Performance improvement identification

#### Learning Outcomes
- Design comprehensive performance measurement systems
- Apply various evaluation methodologies
- Assess alliance success and identify improvements
- Benchmark alliance performance effectively

---

### Module 9: Alliance Termination
**Duration**: 2 weeks  
**Learning Focus**: Alliance conclusion and dissolution management

#### Key Topics
- Termination types and trajectories
- Termination planning and execution
- Asset transfer and intellectual property
- Relationship conclusion and learning capture
- Post-termination strategies

#### Learning Outcomes
- Plan and manage alliance terminations effectively
- Handle asset transfer and IP issues
- Capture learning and value from concluded alliances
- Develop post-termination strategic options
