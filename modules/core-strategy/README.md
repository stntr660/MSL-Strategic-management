# Core Strategy Module

Fundamental strategic management concepts, frameworks, and methodologies.

## Module Overview

**Learning Objectives:**
- Master core strategic analysis frameworks
- Understand competitive intelligence gathering and analysis
- Learn strategic alliance formation and management
- Develop strategic change management capabilities

**Prerequisites:** Basic business knowledge
**Duration:** 8-10 weeks
**Level:** Intermediate to Advanced

## Practical Exercises

### Exercise 1: Competitive Intelligence Report
**Objective:** Conduct competitive analysis for a chosen industry
**Deliverable:** 10-page CI report with recommendations
**Timeline:** 2 weeks

### Exercise 2: Alliance Strategy Proposal
**Objective:** Develop strategic alliance proposal
**Deliverable:** Business case and partnership framework
**Timeline:** 2 weeks

### Exercise 3: Change Management Plan
**Objective:** Design organizational change initiative
**Deliverable:** Comprehensive change plan with timeline
**Timeline:** 2 weeks
